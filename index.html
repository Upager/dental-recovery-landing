FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Is my patient data secure and HIPAA compliant?

+

Yes, absolutely. The software runs entirely on your computer - no patient data is transmitted to external servers. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. We only have read-only access to your PMS database, meaning we cannot modify any patient records. All emails are sent from your own SMTP server using your practice domain.

Which practice management systems are supported?

+

Currently we support Open Dental (both local MySQL and Cloud API versions). We're actively adding support for Denticon, Curve Dental, CareStack, and Archy. Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Practice-Web integrations are coming soon. If your PMS isn't listed, contact us - we're adding new integrations regularly.

How does the 5% commission pricing work?

+

You only pay when you actually collect revenue from recovered treatment. The software tracks which patients scheduled appointments due to recovery emails and calculates 5% of the completed treatment value. We bill monthly for the previous month's collections. If we don't recover any revenue, you pay nothing. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or per-seat licenses.

Will this bother my patients with too many emails?

+

No. The system includes intelligent frequency caps and respects patient preferences. Recovery sequences are spaced appropriately (typically 7-14 days between contacts) and stop automatically when a patient schedules or opts out. All emails include clear unsubscribe options and we maintain an automatic suppression list. The emails are warm, personalized, and focused on helping patients complete treatment they've already said yes to.

How long does installation and setup take?

+

Most practices are up and running in 10-15 minutes. You download the installer, connect to your PMS database (MySQL credentials or API key), configure your email settings (Gmail, Outlook, or any SMTP server), and set your recovery rules. The setup wizard walks you through each step. We also offer free onboarding assistance for the first 30 days.

What happens during the free trial?

+

During your 30-day free trial, the software runs with full functionality. It finds unscheduled treatment, sends recovery emails, tracks responses, and attributes recovered revenue - exactly like the paid version. You can see real results and recovered revenue before paying anything. If you're happy with the results after 30 days, billing starts automatically at 5% of recovered revenue. No credit card required to start.

Can I customize the email templates?

+

Yes! The software includes several pre-built email templates, but you can fully customize them to match your practice's voice and branding. You can edit subject lines, body text, include your logo, and set different templates for different procedures or patient segments. The AI personalization layer works with your templates to make each email feel individually written.

What kind of results can I expect?

+

Most practices recover 15-25% of their unscheduled treatment within the first 90 days. This translates to $50K-$200K in recovered revenue per year for an average-sized practice. Results vary based on how much unscheduled treatment you have, your recovery rules, and patient engagement. The software provides detailed reporting so you can track exactly what's being recovered.
